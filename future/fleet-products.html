  • Contract Hire

    • Did you know: You can recover 50% of VAT on the finance rental

      Whether you're running a single car or a fleet of 10,000 vans, contract hire enables you to control one of the biggest business expenses.

    • The running cost of cars and light commercial vehicles is one of the biggest expenses most businesses face. Add to that the risk of depreciation, and it’s no wonder Contract Hire is such an appealing option.

      What are your key benefits?

      • Vehicles appear off the balance sheet
      • No financial risks associated with vehicle disposals
      • Beneficial Corporation Tax treatment for leased vehicles
      • Convenience of optional bolt on services like maintenance

  • Finance Lease

    • Did you know: You can recover 100% of the VAT on commercial vehicles as long as you're VAT registered

      Finance Lease is a popular funding option for commercial vehicles where Contract Hire is not suitable. (Cars can be financed as well)

    • Monthly rentals can be lowered through the introduction of a balloon rental at the end of the contract. This can be set at a value equivalent to a forecasted residual value or reduced in line with anticipated wear and tear on the used value.

      What are your key benefits?

      • Flexible rentals by customising initial and final payments
      • You recieve 98% of the post contract sales proceeds
      • You can re-finance the final payment if required
      • Finance Lease is a 'Usership' type contract
